Anno 222 A.G

Entry I Hark! I find myself upon the shores of Titania, a land spoken of with much repute. Rumors led mine ears to tales of a grand castle that doth stand as the heart of this realm. Entry II Verily, my quest to locate the castle proved treacherous, for in the mountainous lands of bitter cold and monsters, I found myself stranded for months. Seeking sanctuary within the mountain's bosom, the gods of these peaks bestowed upon me the providence needed for survival. Entry III Having endured the mountain's harsh embrace, I have named it Mount Spectra in homage to mine ancestors,and now, it is mine dwelling. As I prepare to descend to the lower realms of Titania, I do reflect upon the trials faced. Entry IV At last, mine eyes gaze upon the looming silhouette of the castle, a formidable presence visible from afar. With caution as my guide, I approach its gates. Entry V The castle, though grand, reveals an open portal, inviting mine exploration across its hallowed grounds. Entry VI A survey of the castle and its environs unfolds before me, yet the northern lands remain untrodden. Entry VII In my initial observations, I discern the presence of several individuals within the castle walls.Lords, mayhaps, governing this domain. Foremost amongst them is Luis the Frank, the master of the castle, overseeing its expansion. Entry VIII Other lords, in allegiance to Luis the Frank, I spy near the castle's domain. Further investigation is warranted to unveil their identities. Entry IX The lords adjacent to the castle appear to possess lands of their own. To the north, the overseer of Titania's village is the alchemically-inclined Lord, the Master of Reins, whose ways remain shrouded in mystery. Entry X Whispers reach mine ears of a lord hailing from the Squirrel lands, yet the details elude me. His identity and purpose remain obscured. Entry XI A warrior lord, clad in armor resembling that of a frog, captures mine attention. His name, a riddle unsolved, and his dominion unknown in the realms of Titania. Entry XII Having chosen a fertile land to cultivate, I embark on the arduous task of mapping these territories whilst delving into the affairs of the lords. A resource-intensive endeavor, yet a necessary one. Entry XIII Alas, the lords of Titania vanish from the castle, leaving naught but questions lingering in the air. The reason for their disappearance remains an enigma. Have they sailed to distant lands, leaving their dominion unmanned? Entry XIV The "Vanishing of the Lords" marks the year 222 A.G. in the annals of Titania. Evidence suggests the presence of at least two lords dwelling nearby, yet their fates remain unclear. Entry XV Exploring the castle surroundings, I uncover the identity of one lord with a fearsome bovine machine - Johnny the Boy, a lord of potential peril. Entry XVI The expansion of my farm complete, I stumble upon a winding path leading to an abode. Could this be the realm of the mysterious Lord of Squirrels? Entry XVII The path reveals the dwelling of another vanished lord to the southwest, adding another enigma to the unraveling tale. Entry XVIII Entering what may be the elusive Lord of the Squirrels' domain, I find no definitive proof. The search persists. Entry XIX Castle grounds, seemingly deserted, witness the return of Luis the Frank, evidenced by ongoing expansion. A chance for communication presents itself, though my attempts prove futile. Entry XX Months pass with no sign of the lords' return. I extend my territories and fortify my holdings. Entry XXI Upon my return, I discover missing livestock. Further exploration is warranted. Entry XXII Commencing the construction of a perimeter wall, I unearth a path leading northeast from my farm. A potential link to the vanished animals? Entry XXIII A revelation unfolds as I chance upon an edifice, well-kept and stocked. A new lord's domain, it seems, and I set camp to keep vigilant watch. Entry XXIV Despite the lords' continued absence, I fortify my perimeter and intensify illumination. Entry XXV To my surprise, the mysterious neighbor is none other than Lord Semaan, lost in a cave that exits onto my lands. A note reveals his plight and his house affiliation. Entry XXVI Contact established, Lord Semaan extends an invitation, and our fruitful discourse unveils his tale as an explorer stranded in these lands. Entry XXVII As the eve of a new year dawns, I, Gen of House Spectra, claim these lands of Titania as mine own. Yet, much labor remains.

Anno 223 A.G

Entry I Encountering an unknown figure near the castle waters, I seek information about the lords. The man, unidentified, speaks of the Lord of the Squirrels and a vessel under construction. Entry II Observing the vessel's progress, I discern a need for assistance. Infrastructure I provide, offering respite to the builder. Entry III The builder converses with another figure, possibly the elusive Lord of the Squirrels. I approach, seeking proximity to discern the truth. Entry IV The Lord of the Squirrels eludes me, departing once again. His lands remain a mystery. Entry V The drydock's completion aids the boat builder. Returning to my lands, I ponder the lords' fate and potential clues to their abandonment of the castle...
